Starting the API...

Launch Services starts Docker, SearXNG, and the Ember API. Open Ember opens the running app in your browser.

A few things to check:

  • Is Ember already running? Check your taskbar for a terminal window.
  • Did a terminal window with (.venv) stay open? Close it and try again.
  • Is port 8000 in use by something else? Close other servers and retry.

On Mac, you may see a security warning the first time you open Ember. Right-click the app and select Open, then click Open again to bypass Gatekeeper. This is a one-time step.
